TEN facts about Jonathan

Read Time: 2 Minutes

He is Ugandan!

He was born in Kampala, Uganda.

He has 3 siblings, a son (red fluffy fuggler with teeth) named Pierre, and a big dream.

"Jonathan loves concerts!" - Jonathan's friends, 2024

He went to Lohana Academy for Primary School. Afterwards, Jonathan did his UCE exams in St. Henry's College, Kitovu in Masaka, Uganda which was 2 hours away from his home. With an excellent grade of 13 in 8, he managed to get into King's College Budo, a top boarding school which was only 1 hour away, meaning his family could visit more often.

He did extremely well in his UACE exams and got AAA, which got him an offer at the University of Leeds, where he studied Actuarial Mathematics.

After months and what felt like years, he secured a placement year with a niche insurance firm called CLS Risk Solutions, which soon turned into MX Underwriting after an acquisition by Specialist Risk Group - It was his first experience of M&A!

Amazing times were had at MX, but his passion for numbers left him wondering, "What does an analytical career really look like?" To this day, he still would like to know!

In university, Jonathan was active! With a membership in badminton, tennis and dodgeball societies, he was always looking for the next best way to meet different people through a layer of sweat.

NAUR WAYYYY!

With university strikes looming, he managed to secure a 2:1 in his Bachelor's degree including world renowned research in African Climate Change with a recent paper named "Combinations of Hazards. What are the chances of them occuring in the future?"

**WOOHOO!!!**

He currently works within the food industry as a Lead Barista, testing his patience with different customers and having a laugh with his colleagues. At the moment, he would like a seat at the Corporate Table ;o
